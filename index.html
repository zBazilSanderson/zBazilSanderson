Artificial Intelligence

Intelligence is the medium.

I'm majoring in AI at Victoria University drawn to it not just as a field of study, but as a design material. The question I keep returning to: what does it feel like when intelligence disappears into a product and just makes it better?

Academic foundation

Studying machine learning theory, neural architectures, probabilistic reasoning, and intelligent systems design building real intuition for what's happening under the hood, not just how to prompt it.

Building with AI

I integrate AI into the things I make from intelligent search and recommendation systems to generative interfaces. The goal is always the same: make the experience feel effortless.

Design meets intelligence

Coming from both design and engineering gives me a specific lens: I care deeply about how AI features feel, not just what they do. The best ones are invisible you just notice the product got smarter.

What excites me now

Multimodal models, agent systems, and the new interface paradigms they unlock. I follow the research closely and think constantly about what these capabilities mean for what we build next.

About

Obsessed with craft.

I'm a designer and developer based in Wellington, NZ. I believe the best work sits at the intersection of clarity and feeling where everything unnecessary has been removed, and what remains just works.

I come from both sides: I design with an engineer's eye for systems, and I build with a designer's eye for detail. That dual perspective shapes everything I make.
